◌ Tips for the Game ◌

  • Tip 1: Stay Focused. Keep your attention on the game screen at all times. Distractions can lead to mistakes.
  • Tip 2: Master the timing of your movements. Sometimes it's better to wait for the right moment than to rush.
  • Tip 3: Keep a cool head, especially when the game gets intense. Panicking can lead to mistakes.
  • Tip 4: Pay attention to the movement patterns of bullets. Try to predict where they'll go next.
  • Tip 5: Make small, precise movements to avoid bullets. Overreacting can lead to collisions.
