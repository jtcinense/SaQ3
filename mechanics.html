◌ Hard Mode ◌

Press E to start

By pressing the W, A, S, and D keys, the player will start moving in the desired direction. Press the key in the direction you want to go, and your character will continue moving as long as the key is held down.

Each key corresponds to a direction:

  • W: Move forward
  • A: Move towards the Left
  • S: Move backward
  • D: Move towards the right

Watch for bullets coming your way. When you see a bullet, quickly press the keys to move out of its path. Try to guess where the bullet will go and move away from that spot. Keep practicing moving and dodging to get better. The more you play, the easier it will become to avoid the bullets.
